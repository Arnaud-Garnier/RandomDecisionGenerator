About this project



Everyday, millions of people loose time making decisions. It's a fact : a recent study showed that people loose about 5% of their time, a day, making decisions. Brought to a lifetime, we will all loose thousands of hours only thinking about decisions we have to make.

It's a serious issue. Our project is to bring awareness, but also to offer a solution.

The solution is simple : why not let randomness make the decisions for you, while you can relax ?

Thanks to our app, you'll only have to enter the decision you need to make, and you will instantly know what you should do.

Here is a list of the undeniable advantages of our solution :

  • - No waste of time.

  • - Statistically, you could make the right decisions.

  • - You can blame the app for all the bad decisions you would have made anyway.

  • - If you want you can get a more complete system, with extra features (see below for more informations).


Our first additional feature is the slap-machine option. When you try a decision that is too much stupid (in other words, if you do not choose the application decision), the slap machine will remember you how stupid you are.
With this feature, we guarentee a better result while using the app.
